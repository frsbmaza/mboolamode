THE SWANS COLLECTION

The Swans Collection by Boo a la Mode draws its inspiration from the elegance and grace of Truman Capote's Swans, embodying old-world glamour with a modern twist. Crafted with exquisite silks and satins from Lake Como, the collection features timeless dresses in Duchess satin and 100% silk, designed to accentuate the wearer's confidence and personal style.
Each piece, from the CZ Guest's pristine whites to the vibrant hues of the Slim Keith, Babe Paley, and Marella Agnelli dresses, is made-to-order, ensuring sustainability and a tailor-made fit. With a focus on enduring elegance, Boo a la Mode's Swans Collection is a tribute to the art of living, creating signature pieces that promise to be cherished for a lifetime.
